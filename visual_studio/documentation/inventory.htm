Most nodes are complete but need testing.  There are a few known bugs.  The nodes that are not implemented are: FontStyle, MovieTexture, Fog, Script, Collision and Text.

Known Bugs and other notes:

(1) Nodes with 'url' fields may allow certain invalid 'URL's to be read, for example DOS filenames.
(2) Proximity sensor and LOD ignores browser viewpoint orientation (ie they only work with default orienation)
(3) Primitive geometry nodes (ie.Box, Cone, Cylinder and Sphere) ignore textureTransform
(4) Sensors and Interpolators are not rendered.  That's why they are N/A.
